About us

The name ProteaSkill draws its inspiration from the South African national flower, the Protea. This remarkable flower embodies the symbolism of the phoenix bird, capable of surviving forest fires and emerging from the flames stronger than before. We named our company on it as it resonates with our mission. At ProteaSkill, we aim to empower students to rise above obstacles and soar towards their dreams. Just as the Protea flower represents resilience and growth, we are dedicated to fostering these qualities in our students, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in any circumstance. Join us on this transformative journey, where education is a powerful tool for growth and success

Our Founder

ProteaSkill is an ambitious dream of Ambadi S Nair, who is passionate about education and technology. With a diverse background that includes a Master's degree in Computer Science and a Bachelor's degree in Physical Education, our founder Ambadi S Nair has collectively spent over seven years in the field of education, both in offline and in the dynamic online learning environment. Her journey led to a vision; to create an educational platform where coding is not just a skill, but an essential tool for thriving in the modern world. We believe that technology is the future, and by equipping individuals with coding skills, we are empowering them to excel in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Join us on this exciting journey as we empower individuals to thrive in a digital-centric world through the art of coding.
